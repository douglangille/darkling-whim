# Writing

Creative fiction projects, story universes, and writing craft resources.

## Structure

- **Story Projects**: Individual folders for each story universe and novel project
- **writing-workflows/**: AI-assisted writing processes, publishing workflows, craft resources
- **archives/**: Historical writing content and Substack backups  
- **tumblr/**: Social media writing archives
- **01_Writing_Creative/** & **11_Personal_Misc/**: Organizational categories

## Content Flow

- **_drafts/**: Active works in progress
- **_writing/**: Completed projects and cold storage for story development
- **_posts/**: Published content ready for website deployment

This domain encompasses both the creative output (stories, universes, characters) and the craft/process knowledge (workflows, techniques, tools) that support your fiction writing.
