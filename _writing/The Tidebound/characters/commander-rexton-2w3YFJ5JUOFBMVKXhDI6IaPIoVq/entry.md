**Commander Rexton (Antagonist) - Aqualex Officer**
- **Role:** Ruthless and ambitious commander of the Aqualex operation seeking to exploit the cove's resources.
- **Physical Attributes:**
  - Tall and imposing figure, with a stern, unyielding expression.
  - Impeccably groomed, reflecting his position of authority.
  - Cybernetic enhancements, subtly integrated into his body, hinting at Aqualex's technological prowess.
- **Manner of Speech:**
  - Authoritative, commanding, and often condescending.
  - Uses technical jargon and corporate buzzwords.
  - Capable of charm when necessary but quick to resort to threats.
- **Dress:**
  - Formal Aqualex uniform, emphasizing rank and authority.
  - Clean, polished boots.
  - Often wears a high-tech visor or communication device.
- **Myers-Briggs:** ENTJ (The Commander)
  - Driven, ambitious, and decisive.
  - Natural leader, but can be ruthless and manipulative.
  - Values efficiency and control above all else.
- **Nova Scotia Inspiration:** Represents the negative aspects of unchecked corporate power and resource extraction, echoing historical exploitation of natural resources in the region. His ambition and ruthlessness contrast with the Tide Keepers' respect for nature.
