# Chapter 7 — Echoes on Constitution Avenue
## Scene Briefs

**Purpose:** Ground the metaphysical thread in blue-collar skepticism; introduce Dunne's quiet conscience.

---

## Scene 1 — Tunnel Inspection (S)

**Location:** Federal Triangle maintenance tunnels  
**POV:** Agent Rafe Dunne  
**Goal:** Conduct routine security sweep of East Wing demolition tunnels

**Obstacle:** Equipment glitches; maintenance crew reports "weird acoustics"

**Conflict/Action:** Dunne uses handheld sensor; readings spike near vault coordinates

**Climax/Turn:** Hears low hum through concrete—no mechanical source visible

**Outcome:** Logs it as "acoustic anomaly"; instinct says otherwise

**Tone/Mood:** Industrial fluorescence, wet concrete, echo of distant machinery

**Symbol:** The city's subconscious leaking through cracks

---

## Sequel 1 — Procedural Doubt (Q)

**Emotional Reaction:** Unease he can't name; trained not to believe in ghosts

**Reflection:** Recalls Mercer's intensity; wonders if the historian knows more

**Decision:** Keep private copy of readings; violates protocol quietly

**Exit Beat:** Returns to surface; sirens echo in rhythm with the hum

**Mood:** Loyal pragmatism eroding into curiosity

---

## Scene 2 — Coffee with Mercer (S)

**Location:** Diner near National Mall  
**POV:** Rafe Dunne  
**Goal:** Casual debrief; gauge Mercer's state of mind

**Obstacle:** Mercer deflects, plays academic

**Conflict/Action:** Dunne mentions the hum offhand; Mercer's mask slips

**Climax/Turn:** They lock eyes—both know something's wrong

**Outcome:** Alliance forms wordlessly; neither admits belief yet

**Tone/Mood:** Diner booth, low light, guarded language

**Symbol:** Trust forming in silence

---

## Sequel 2 — Dunne Alone (Q)

**Emotional Reaction:** Feels the pull of something larger than duty

**Reflection:** Wonders if his job is to protect the building or the people

**Decision:** Monitor Mercer informally; stay close

**Exit Beat:** Drives past the White House; sees construction lights forming geometric lattice

**Mood:** Quiet resolve; soldier becoming guardian

---

## Writing Notes

- Dunne voice: blue-collar directness, gallows humor
- Establish working-class skepticism meeting inexplicable evidence
- Show loyalty being tested by instinct
- Partnership forming through shared doubt, not shared belief
