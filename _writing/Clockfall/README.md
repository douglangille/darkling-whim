# Clockfall

**Genre**: Neo-noir procedural with alt-history speculative realism  
**Setting**: Post-Blackout Near-Future Nova Scotia (2032)  
**Status**: Development - World Building Complete

## Project Overview

Clockfall is a grounded techno-noir story set in Nova Scotia, five years after a global "Time Blackout" event that fractured digital trust and forced society to rebuild with analog systems. The story follows Richard Grayson (RCMP forensic time integrity consultant) and Maggie Kincaid (independent horologist/Clock Mistress) as they investigate temporal anomalies tied to a martyred systems architect named Marco.

## Tone & Style

- **Influences**: *The Leftovers*, *True Detective S1*, *Watchmen* (TV), *Mr. Robot*
- **Aesthetic**: Wet concrete, analog signal fuzz, flickering sodium lights
- **Dialogue**: Minimalist, loaded with subtext, sharp but human
- **No comic book tropes** - Mythic subtext without costumes or codenames

## Timeline

- **1963**: McNabs Island temporal anomaly (Cold War flashback)
- **2026**: The Decency Case (Richard & Marco investigate tampered timestamps)
- **Early 2027**: Marco's descent and research into temporal drift
- **May 11, 2027**: Marco's death at Halifax airport / Time Blackout begins
- **2028-2031**: The Burning Years (reconstruction)
- **2032**: Maggie discovers Marco's heartbeat signature / Frame story begins
