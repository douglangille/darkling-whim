### Tonal Guidelines - No Comic Book Tropes

**Critical Design Decision:**
"Never overtly name Nightwing or Clock Mistress. We don't costume them. At best these are screen handles or hacker aliases, but maybe not even that. The secret alter ego is not the focus of the story, but its subtext."

**Influence Palette:**
- Tone: The Leftovers, True Detective (S1), Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy, The Lives of Others
- Structure: Watchmen (TV) meets Mr. Robot
- Aesthetic: Wet concrete, analog signal fuzz, flickering sodium lights
- Dialogue: Minimalist, loaded with subtext, sharp but human
- Romantic Tension: Like The Americans or Before Sunrise, but haunted

**Character Archetypes (Unspoken):**
- Richard: The Watcher, The Fallen Knight
- Maggie: The Tinker, The Prophetess
- Marco: The Martyr, The Trickster
- Antagonist: The Editor (not a person - a system, feedback loop, force that decides what time is)

### Marco's Death - The Inciting Incident

**May 11, 2027 - Halifax Stanfield International Airport**

**Marco's Plan:**
- Breach secure maintenance zone with analog oscillator rig
- Inject "temporal correction pulse" into airport's hyper-synced comms
- Halifax is keystone node in Canadian EastNet timing relay
- Hypothesis: Analog heartbeat can collapse AI feedback spike

**The Accident (Public):**
- Runway incursion
- Malfunctioning service gate
- Bizarre systems error
- Caught in jet engine intake - shredded instantly
- Security footage lost
- Access logs deleted in Blackout

**The Truth (Richard's Knowledge):**
- Pocketwatch recovered from crash site - ticking, out of phase
- Manual journal left 12 hours earlier
- Final line: "If I miss, bury me in the gap. Time won't forgive me, but maybe you will."
- Residual sync pulses still echoing in fringe networks

**Symbolic Weight:**
- Visceral, symbolic, deeply grounded death
- Martyr who wasn't saintly
- Sacrifice was heroic - or reckless - or both
