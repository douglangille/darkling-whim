    Up your game with images in three easy steps:

    1. Pick a colour palette from Adobe or color-hex.
    2. Search TinEye Labs for neat Flickr images.
    3. Create a spiffy social media image with Buffer’s Pablo.

    • Search Flickr for Creative Commons pictures.
