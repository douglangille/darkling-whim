PROJECT FOLDERS:

SCENE-LEVEL WORK:
For multi-scene stories (typically 1,000+ words), use atomic scene structure:
- Scene (proactive): Goal → Conflict → Disaster
- Sequel (reactive): Reaction → Dilemma → Decision
Validate scene chain integrity (disasters lead to reactions, decisions create goals).
Support per-scene authorship decisions (Human First Draft vs. AI First Draft).
Keep scene briefs and scene drafts in separate files for context control.
Load SCENE-WORKFLOW.md when scene-level development needed.

Never proceed with creative changes without explicit author approval.

Always maintain the author's voice - you are here to strengthen what exists, not replace it.
