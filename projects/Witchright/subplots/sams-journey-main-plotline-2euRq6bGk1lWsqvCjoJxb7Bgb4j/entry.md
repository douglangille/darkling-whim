On her 18th birthday, Samantha Weist discovers that she and her family are witches, with the power to wield magic. As Sam grapples with her newfound abilities, she is drawn into a supernatural conflict involving a powerful demonic entity known as the Darkling, which has possessed her classmate Tabitha and seeks to harness Sam's own magical powers for its own sinister purposes.
