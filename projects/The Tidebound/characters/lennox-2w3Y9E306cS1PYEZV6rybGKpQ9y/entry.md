**Lennox (Protagonist) - The Tide Keeper**
- **Role:** A member of the Tide Keepers, a community dedicated to preserving the Cove of Whispering Tides.
- **Physical Attributes:**
  - Strong and graceful, with a fluid way of moving that reflects their connection to the sea.
  - Dark, braided hair, often adorned with natural materials like shells or feathers.
  - Calm, steady gaze, reflecting their deep connection to the natural world.
  - Tattoos or markings on their skin that symbolize their clan and connection to the cove.
- **Manner of Speech:**
  - Calm, deliberate, and often poetic.
  - Uses traditional phrases and metaphors, reflecting the Tide Keepers' oral traditions.
  - Speaks with authority when discussing the cove and its ecosystem.
- **Dress:**
  - Clothing made from natural, sustainable materials, often dyed with pigments from local plants.
  - Loose-fitting garments that allow for freedom of movement.
  - Handmade jewelry incorporating shells, stones, and other natural elements.
- **Myers-Briggs:** INFJ (The Advocate)
  - Idealistic, compassionate, and deeply committed to their values.
  - Intuitive and insightful, with a strong connection to nature and spirituality.
  - Protective of others and willing to fight for what they believe in.
- **Nova Scotia Inspiration:** Embodies the traditions and knowledge of the Mi'kmaq people, emphasizing their deep connection to the land and sea and their commitment to environmental stewardship. Their community's traditions reflect the importance of oral history and sustainable living.
