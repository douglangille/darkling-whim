# The Third Faction
by Doug Langille

## The Setup

### Chapter 1: Yrnia Flyby

Start with first atmospheric generator explosion and impact

### Chapter 2: Jak in the Box

Turns off TV, At the office, paperwork, calls Kara, argues

### Chapter 3: Princess Pouts

Kara complains to Monarch about traditions, current obsession with peace, her shitty marriage to Jak. He talks of the weight of the crown

### Chapter 4: Thumbscrews

News broadcast about authoritarian beatdown & protests.

### Chapter 5: Little Ditty

Jak meets new coworker Diane, flirts, go for a drink

### Chapter 6: Report

Diane reports to Huma Proper, gets orders

### Chapter 7: Glimpse

Diane tells Jak about the Third Faction, asks him to join, shows a glimpse of it

### Chapter 8: Scorned

Kara discovers the affair, is embarrassed/furious, Monarch says it's normal, she calls Jak but hangs up before chat

### Chapter 9: Confront

Kara confront's Diane about the affair and surprisingly Diane admits to just using Jak with the fictitious Third Faction. Romantic sparks

### Chapter 10: Proposal

Diane proposes a plan to solve Kara's problems with Monarch and Jak and save people

### Chapter 11: Patronizing

HP propaganda - patronizing

### Chapter 12: Rendezvous

Jak and Diane rendevous. She convinces him to blow up the remaining atmospheric generator. Jak talks about Yrn's ancient war mongering ways

### Chapter 13: Sabotage

Jak and team set explosives

### Chapter 14: Explosion

Explosion, city is plunged into darkness, Diane plants evidence

### Chapter 15: Patricide

Under the cover of dark, Kara murders her father

### Chapter 16: Regent

Kara is installed as Regent, blames explosion on Huma Proper, declares war.

### Chapter 17: Plea

Jak pleads with Diane for asylum. Diane agrees if he kills Kara. Promises a future where they rule a unified people together under Third Faction banner
