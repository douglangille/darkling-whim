**1. Kaelen (Protagonist) - The Scavenger**
- **Role:** A resourceful and independent scavenger who discovers the Cove of Whispering Tides.
- **Physical Attributes:**
  - Lean and agile build, adapted to navigating treacherous terrain.
  - Tanned skin, weathered from exposure to the elements.
  - Intense, observant eyes, constantly scanning her surroundings.
  - Close-cropped, dark, wavy hair, practical for her lifestyle.
  - Several scars on her hands and arms from scavenging.
- **Manner of Speech:**
  - Direct, concise, and often sarcastic.
  - Uses local slang and idioms, reflecting her familiarity with the coastal communities.
  - Speaks quickly when excited or under pressure.
- **Dress:**
  - Functional, layered clothing made from salvaged materials, reinforced with repurposed fishing gear.
  - Waterproof boots with good grip.
  - A multi-purpose belt with tools and pouches.
  - Often wears a repurposed fisherman's hat or hood to protect herself from the elements.
- **Myers-Briggs:** ISTP (The Virtuoso)
  - Practical, adaptable, and skilled at problem-solving.
  - Independent and reserved but capable of quick action in a crisis.
  - Values freedom and dislikes rigid rules or authority.
- **Nova Scotia Inspiration:** Reflects the resilience and self-reliance of those who have historically lived on the margins, adapting to harsh conditions and making use of what they can find. Her knowledge of tides and currents echoes the importance of the sea in Nova Scotia's culture.
