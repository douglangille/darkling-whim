<img src="/assets/images/ec3cb80a2af51c3e81584d04ee44408be273e7d119b3174792f8_640_baking.jpg">Moldy sugar and weevily flour,  
 Some rancid butter at the Witching Hour.  
 Wriggly worms, spiders and flies,  
 Spooky cookies, a treater's surprise.
