## The 4 Haley Theories (Shyamalan Mystery Framework)

Weave **all four** ambiguously until Act 3 convergence. Plant evidence progressively.

### Theory 1: Immune/Carrier Hybrid

- **Evidence**: Passed tests ("They cleared me"), survives bites/scratches.
- **Implication**: Valuable for vaccine; dangerous vector. Spooks want biology extraction.
- **Clues**: Wounds heal oddly; zombie fluid "touches" her differently.[^1]


### Theory 2: Latent Controller / Queen of Zombies

- **Evidence**: Eerie calm around hordes; zombies hesitate/protect her; naming rituals.
- **Implication**: Instinctive influence → conscious command (pheromone/psychic).
- **Queen Meanings**:

1. Literal ruler (commands herds).
2. Spiritual shepherd (honors dead).
3. Tragic irony (trapped figurehead).
4. Transformation (post-human archetype).[^1]


### Theory 3: Sacrifice Portal Figure

- **Evidence**: Fire compulsion (burns hospital/mother's house); isolation despite crew.
- **Implication**: Beacon/attractor for plague; spooks want to amplify/suppress via her.
- **Climax**: Self-immolation choice to stop outbreak.[^1]


### Theory 4: Hybrid Child / Post-Human Evolution

- **Evidence**: Child/lethal split; no sleep needed; eyes reflect light; senses zombies.
- **Implication**: Contagion + trauma → new sentient form bridging human/zombie.
- **Climax**: Hive consciousness merger; negotiates coexistence.[^1]
