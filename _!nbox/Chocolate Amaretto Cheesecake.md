# Chocolate Amaretto Cheesecake (Stabilized NY-Style)

### Ingredients

**Crust**
* 1 cup crushed graham wafers
* 3 tbsp butter, melted

**Filling**
* 26 oz (3 pkgs) cream cheese, room temperature
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1.5 tbsp cornstarch
* 3 eggs, room temperature
* 1/2 tsp amaretto
* 1/2 cup chocolate chips
* 3 tbsp coffee cream

**Glaze**
* 3/4 cup cream
* 1 cup chocolate chipits

---

### Instructions

**1. Preparation**
* Clamp parchment paper into the bottom of a 10" springform pan. Do not grease walls.
* Place a metal 13x9 pan on the bottom oven rack.
* Combine crust ingredients, press into pan, and pre-bake at 400°F for 10 minutes.

**2. Mixing**
* Whisk cornstarch into the sugar until no lumps remain.
* Melt 1/2 cup chips with 3 tbsp cream; stir in amaretto. Cool to lukewarm.
* Blend cream cheese and sugar/starch mixture until smooth.
* Add eggs one at a time on low speed; mix just until incorporated.
* Hand-fold the lukewarm chocolate mixture into the batter using a spatula.

**3. Baking**
* Fill the 13x9 pan on the bottom rack with 1 inch of boiling water.
* Pour batter over crust. Bake at 400°F for 10 minutes.
* Reduce heat to 250°F and bake for 50–60 minutes until the 3-inch center is barely firm/jiggly.

**4. Cooling & Release**
* Remove from oven and immediately run a knife around the sides.
* Return to the turned-off oven with the door cracked for 1 hour.
* Chill in the refrigerator for at least 6–8 hours.

**5. Finishing**
* Simmer 3/4 cup cream and stir in 1 cup chipits until melted.
* Spread over the completely cooled cake while still in the pan.
* Let glaze set before removing the springform ring.
