Creative quotes and quotations
On effort...

 

Nobody said that being creative was a walk in the park. Sometimes you have dig and dig for the initial spark. Sometimes the difficult bit is developing the idea into something workable. In any case, be prepared to put your shoulder where your mouth is and keep on bashing until you get through to the other side.
