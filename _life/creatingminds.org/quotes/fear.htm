On fear...

 

You’ve got to feel the fear and do it anyway, in the immortal words of a popular book. Fear can a great stimulation, but it is more often a block, both within the minds of would-be creators and their more vocal critics. The fear of change, of things new leads to resistance and blockages that hinder any creative new idea.
