Creative quotes and quotations
By Experts...

 

What makes an expert an expert? Their knowledge of the past. They know what used to work in extreme detail. In fact they have built a career and a reputation that expertise. Now put a new idea in front of the expert. Are they going to declare themselves completely invalid? Or are they going to puff themselves up and make complete idiots out of them for all of history to see?
