small words, BIG CHANGE: the series!

 

 

How to subtly influence and persuade with the smallest of words

Buy at Amazon: USA | UK | France | Deutschland | Italia | España | Japan | Brasil
 

Do you need to influence, persuade or otherwise change the minds of other people? Sure you do. Everybody does. Whether it is in work, at home or out there in the big world, we all want others to do things, including when they are not so sure that they want to go along with our requests.

The 'small words, BIG CHANGE' series comes from David Straker, the author of 'Changing minds: In Detail' and the massive changingminds.org website. These book look at the practicalities of how you can use the smallest of words to subtly influence and persuade. While doing so, they also discuss many tips, tricks and things to consider when you are nudging and communicating with others.

Where can I buy them?

It's waiting for you in your country's Amazon Kindle stores. Click your link below or search for 'small words, BIG CHANGE' in any Amazon web.

How much are they?

Each book is either $5 or something close in your local currency, plus any local tax. This is a reduction! The first book was originally $7, but the price has been reduced for now so you can easily buy the set.

How big are they?

The number of pages in a book is not always related to its value. There is solid information on every page that explains and shows how to practically use each of the small words discussed. Pages are also a variable quantity in e-books. For practical comparison, however, the books can be described as being around 100 pages.

Can I buy them in an other format?

Not at the moment. We're starting with Kindle and may provide other formats if the demand is sufficient.
