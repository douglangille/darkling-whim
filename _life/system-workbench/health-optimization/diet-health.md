## Doug's Default Day OS

### Design Principles

- **Not a "diet"** â€” a default operating system
- Target: ~80% adherence most days
- Low-friction
- Flexible
- Neutral awareness (not scorekeeping)

### Breakfast (Solo, Autopilot)

**Omelet (no bread)**:
- Sets daily tone
- Anchors the system
- Bread dropped intentionally

### Lunch

**Protein + Veg**:
- Portion control
- Bulk with vegetables

### Supper

**50/25/25 Plate Ratio**:
- 50% vegetables
- 25% protein
- 25% starch/carbs
- Include pickles (abundant garden supply)

### Evening Snack

**Structured, Approved Combo**:
- Crunch + salt + protein + drink
- Reframed from "guilt trigger" to "approved ritual"

### Store-Run Protocol

**Guardrails** (not confession):
- Ritual approach
- Private tracking
- Prevents secret junk food buys

### Food Logging

**Neutral Fitbit Logging**:
- Process, not scoreboard
- Awareness tool
- Reviewed weekly for trends (not daily judgment)

### Grocery Focus List

- Structured shopping
- Aligned with Default Day components
