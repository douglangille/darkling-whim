## Strategic Decisions

### Tales & Tangents Naming

**Navigation label:** Tales & Tangents  
**Actual site name:** On a Darkling Whim  
**Domain:** douglangille.ca

**Rationale:** "Tales & Tangents" describes function better than poetic site title. About page uses "Tales & Tangents (douglangille.ca)" to smooth transition for readers who click through.

### Separation Strategy Clarification

Analysis revealed "soft separation" approach:
- Content strictly separated (no professional posts on Darkling, no fiction on Digital Doug)
- But navigation acknowledges connection (Tales & Tangents link, footer references)
- Allows interested readers to discover full range without forcing it
- Maintains professional credibility while avoiding identity hiding

### Platform Terminology

**Use "newsletter"** not "Substack"
- Newsletter = what it is (format/medium)
- Substack = where it's hosted (platform)
- Avoids platform lock-in language
