# Chapter 8 — The Leak
## Scene Briefs

**Purpose:** Break containment; accelerate chaos; force Mercer into public scrutiny.

---

## Scene 1 — Kells Publishes (S)

**Location:** *The Atlantic* newsroom / social media  
**POV:** Dorian Kells  
**Goal:** Drop exposé on the vault cipher

**Obstacle:** Editorial hesitation; "too conspiracy-flavored"

**Conflict/Action:** Kells leaks images to social media instead; virality instant

**Climax/Turn:** Hashtag #WashingtonSigil trends within hours

**Outcome:** Mainstream news forced to cover it

**Tone/Mood:** Screen glow, scrolling feeds, frenzy

**Symbol:** Truth as contagion; information uncontained

---

## Sequel 1 — Public Reaction Montage (Q)

**POV:** Multiple (brief clips)  
**Emotional Reaction:** Fascination, mockery, fear—spectrum of belief

**Reflection:** Armchair theorists decode patterns; some eerily accurate

**Decision:** Federal agencies issue "no comment" holding pattern

**Exit Beat:** Image of the vault goes from niche to ubiquitous overnight

**Mood:** Acceleration; story slipping from anyone's control

---

## Scene 2 — Mercer in the Storm (S)

**Location:** Mercer's apartment  
**POV:** Cal Mercer  
**Goal:** Evade media; maintain professional cover

**Obstacle:** Reporters swarm his apartment; colleagues avoid him

**Conflict/Action:** Vale shields him via institutional channels; Cartwright calls emergency meeting

**Climax/Turn:** Mercer realizes he's no longer a scholar—he's evidence

**Outcome:** Forced into temporary "research sabbatical"

**Tone/Mood:** Phone buzzing, camera flashes through blinds, isolation

**Symbol:** Exposure as exile

---

## Sequel 2 — Vale's Intervention (Q)

**POV:** Miriam Vale  
**Emotional Reaction:** Protectiveness mixed with anger at his recklessness

**Reflection:** She knows this pattern; it consumed others before

**Decision:** Reach out to Cartwright; invoke old alliance

**Exit Beat:** Vale closes her office door; faint hum audible through walls

**Mood:** Mentor stepping into danger to save student

---

## Writing Notes

- Rapid pacing—multiple short scenes
- Show viral spread through sensory overload (screens, noise, notifications)
- Mercer's isolation deepens
- Vale's protectiveness foreshadows her eventual sacrifice
- Information warfare: both factions lose control of narrative
