# World Bible
## The Invisible Rite

---

## I. Core Mythos

**Premise:**  
Washington, D.C. was designed not only as a capital but as a **geometric invocation** — a city-wide Masonic sigil meant to align human will with divine order. Over two centuries, that symbolic architecture has been corrupted, fragmented, and reinterpreted by rival secret lineages.

The "Invisible Rite" is the esoteric war for who controls that geometry — and through it, America's soul.

---

## II. Factions

### 1. The Heritage Order

**Public Face:** The Heritage Foundation and its policy ecosystem.  
**Secret Origin:** Founded in 1935 by Masonic traditionalists and early Cold War technocrats seeking to bind divine hierarchy to modern governance.  
**Creed:** "Order is the shape of freedom."  
**Goal:** Complete the Washington Sigil — an architectural and bureaucratic convergence that permanently aligns U.S. institutions under their spiritual authority.

**Methods:**
- Embedding symbolic geometry in federal construction projects
- Writing legislation as ritual structures ("Invocation Acts")
- Cultivating figureheads whose charisma amplifies ritual energy

**Key Figures:**
- **Nathan Voss** — The Wordsmith. Legal savant turned metaphysical linguist. Treats executive orders as sacred texts.
- **Evelyn March** — The Steward. Economic mystic managing the invisible tithes that sustain the Order.
- **President Arlen Cross** — The Vessel. A populist showman unaware he's the ritual's keystone.

### 2. The Democratic Rite

**Public Face:** A loose network of reformists, academics, and technocrats claiming lineage from Jeffersonian rationalism.  
**Secret Origin:** A Masonic offshoot formed after 1804 when Jefferson's disciples broke from Federalist ritual orthodoxy.  
**Creed:** "Light belongs to all, but burns who holds it."  
**Goal:** Preserve the Republic's rational flame and prevent metaphysical capture. But internal rot—elitism, data manipulation, moral decay—has compromised them.

**Methods:**
- Information control and public myth-making
- Quietly erasing architectural symbols from key civic buildings
- Funding the Continuity Archives, a hidden library under the Library of Congress

**Key Figure:**
- **Dr. Miriam Vale** — Senior archivist and Mercer's mentor. Torn between truth and containment.
- **Senator Lila Cartwright** — Pragmatic guardian; believes secrecy preserves stability.

### 3. The Custodians

A third, shadowy order of Masons tracing back to Washington's original surveyors.  
**Creed:** The geometry must never be completed.  
They are scattered hermits, builders, and janitors — quietly sabotaging both factions. Mercer's father was one.

**Key Figure:**
- **Brother Null** — Last known active Custodian; maintenance worker beneath National Archives.

## V. Historical Timeline (Condensed)

**1791:** L'Enfant and Ellicott lay out Washington D.C.'s geometric plan under Masonic oversight. Design embeds ritual sigil of "Republican Harmony."

**1804:** Jefferson schism creates the Democratic Rite. Original sigil left incomplete.

**1935:** Mellon Auditorium consecration; Heritage Order secretly forms.

**1942–45:** PEOC built as wartime bunker, aligned precisely with unfinished sigil coordinates.

**1983:** Reagan administration rediscovers the Charter; fragments integrated into "Star Wars" defense program schematics.

**2002:** PEOC expansion under post-9/11 modernization quietly realigns key nodes.

**2025:** The Ballroom Project begins — intended to "complete the geometry."

## VIII. The "Magic" System

**Architectural Resonance:**
- Masonic design acts as metaphysical circuitry
- Speech and ritual can trigger geometric activation
- Words (logos) and structures (form) share harmonic law
- No overt supernatural entities; metaphysics framed as emergent physics or "faith-tech"

**The Custodians' Rule:**  
Perfection completes the circuit → annihilation of free will.  
Imperfection preserves freedom.

1. **The Visible City** — Public, daylight democracy; rational facade
2. **The Hidden City** — Tunnels, PEOC, bureaucratic underworld
3. **The Unseen City** — Metaphysical echo; geometry humming beneath consciousness

The story moves through all three, with each act descending one layer deeper.
