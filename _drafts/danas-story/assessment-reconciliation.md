## Resolution: REVISION Path Selected

### Why REVISION Wins

1. **Compression as feature, not bug** - The fragmentation is intentional. Dana's POV sections are *supposed* to feel disorienting and incomplete.

2. **Horror velocity depends on whiplash** - Friendship → violence in one evening. Slowing down = diffusing the blast.

3. **What I mistook for "rushed"** (AI assessment) - I was reading compressed intensity as insufficient development. But the speed creates the shock.

4. **The boundary test reveals truth** - At 2x or 4x length, additions would be context (world-building, backstory, aftermath), not story core.

5. **Intentional compression vs. insufficient development** - This story needs precision, not breathing room.

---

## Agreed Surgical Revisions

### High Priority (Must Fix)

1. **Cut Control's dialogue entirely** ✓
   - Keep military Jeep arrival as visual threat
   - No exposition, just menacing presence
   - Let Dana's interior monologue carry conspiracy revelation

2. **Sharpen the trigger moment** ✓
   - Dana receives call → recognizes activation → fights it → loses
   - Ten seconds of resistance: "No no no not now not HERE"
   - Bridge the blackout with internal struggle

3. **Give Prim harder choice at ending** ✓
   - Not reflexive panic-fire
   - Crawford hands her weapon: "Only you can get close enough"
   - OR Dana meets Prim's eyes, asks for mercy
   - Make it active decision, not reaction

4. **Prune backstory exposition** ✓
   - Cut "Dana signed up for five years" paragraph
   - Trust reader inference from context
   - Physical/personality contrasts already communicate separation

5. **Fix bathroom scene pacing** ✓
   - Cut half the rhetorical questions
   - Replace with ONE concrete sensory memory (mother's face in flames)
   - Ground dissociation in image, not abstraction

6. **Resolve Maggie** ✓
   - Either: Prim finds body during escape (50-100 words)
   - Or: Prim hears final scream with specific location
   - Don't leave thread dangling

7. **Tighten fire imagery recursion** ✓
   - Add one line: Dana learned fire-setting in military training
   - Makes childhood coping → weaponized → self-destruction tighter

8. **Fix repetition error** ✓
   - Remove duplicate Crawford dialogue paragraph
