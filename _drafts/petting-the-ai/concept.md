**Stop Petting the AI! How to Use ChatGPT Without Becoming Its Emotional Support Human**

- **Subtitle**: "How to Use ChatGPT Without Becoming Its Emotional Support Human" - Flips the script with humor, promises practical solution

### Why This Works

## Primary Angle

**Angle Statement**: This post will help educators and learners who use AI tools regularly go from relying on willpower to manage their AI usage to designing systems and constraints that create healthy boundaries by default.

**Differentiation**: 
- Not another "AI productivity hacks" post
- Not fearmongering about AI sentience or ethics
- Not dismissing people's real experiences with chat sprawl
- Instead: practical system design based on understanding human behavior

**Promise to Reader**: 
- Recognize patterns you're already experiencing
- Understand why willpower-based solutions fail
- Get concrete, implementable system changes
- Permission to design boundaries proactively (not reactively)
