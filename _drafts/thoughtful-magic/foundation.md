## Expand (Development Targets)

### New Scenes Needed
1. **The City Prologue (New)**: Show Amy nearly caught by the "wisecracker" in a heist that goes south because she *wasn't* being deliberate enough.
2. **The Lair Planning (New)**: A scene with Stacey debating the "vacation" vs "operation."
3. **The Infiltration (New)**: Amy bypassing the estate's high-tech security, demonstrating her "thoughtful" superpower.

### Character Development
- **Stacey (Fembot)**: Expand her from a suggestion-bot to a sarcastic, literal-minded logic foil for Amy.
- **The Rivalry**: Establish the specific history of their "awkward courtship" through dialogue subtext.

### World-Building
- **Atlas Ethics**: Define what her "own brand of ethics" actually entails.
- **Winter-Lair Detail**: Flesh out the contrast between "mega-evil" tech and Caribbean luxury.
